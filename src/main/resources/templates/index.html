About TechTrek...

Landing your first Software Developer role can be daunting. TechTrek was developed by web development graduates to provide job placement resources in a user-friendly, straightforward format.

For placement team members, TechTrek provides a simple tool to keep your job-seeking students and staff members informed of resume changes and career status.

Functionality...

  • Resumes
  • Job Listings
  • Tech Events

Your resume is a critical component of your transition to tech. Highlighting your skills, projects, and prior experience will help you stand out from the crowd. Upload and receive feedback easily from your placement team with TechTrek's Resume Portal.

Never miss a job opportunity with TechTrek's job listings page! Populated by your placement team, TechTrek only shows openings relevant to your cohort's qualifications. Apply quickly and effectively with minimal filtering.

Stay in the loop with TechTrek's event listings! Find out essential information regarding upcoming Tech Talks, career fairs, networking opportunities, interest groups and more at the click of a mouse.
